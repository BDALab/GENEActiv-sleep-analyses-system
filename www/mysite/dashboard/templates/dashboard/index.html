Welcome to GENEActiv data processing website

A website created as Master thesis

This site was created to manage study about sleep disorders using GENEActiv actigraph and to present its results. But the template could be used pretty well to manage any study, even yours. There is the Dashboard, that presents the results of the study in a friendly way. And there is the Administration site. There you can manage the subjects of study, upload the data etc.
